About Us

Welcome to our Gala Dinner website! We are excited to celebrate this special event with you.

At Heriot-Watt University, we are delighted to celebrate the accomplishments of our graduating students with a memorable evening of dining and camaraderie.

We believe that education is a powerful tool that can transform lives and shape a better future.

About HappyU

HappyU is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting and empowering students in their educational journey.

Our mission is to provide access to quality education and create opportunities for students from diverse backgrounds.

Why a Dinner?

The Gala Dinner is a culmination of our students' hard work and perseverance. It serves as a platform to honor their achievements, recognize their dedication, and celebrate their successful completion of their educational journey. It is a momentous occasion for our students, their families, and the entire foundation community.
